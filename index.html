Shaping the Future of Tax Policy

The Chartered Institute of Taxation (Kenya) advances the understanding and development of tax policy and law through rigorous research, training, and strategic dissemination of knowledge.

About CITK

The Chartered Institute of Taxation (Kenya) is a non-partisan, not-for-profit institution established to support the development of fair, sustainable, and effective tax systems. Our goal is to reinforce revenue mobilization, spur innovation, and enhance social equity to support broader economic growth.

We serve as a structured platform for professionals—lawyers, accountants, economists, and other stakeholders interested in taxation—to collaborate on research projects, share insights through seminars and publications, and contribute to informed tax policy discussions and engagement. Our diverse network ensures that tax policy discussions benefit from multiple perspectives and expertise.

Our Objective

To provide high-quality services with integrity, professionalism, transparency, reliability, and sustainability across the Global South.
