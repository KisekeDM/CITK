WHAT WE DO

Advancing expertise, shaping policy, and championing equity in taxation.

Training

The Institute offers customized training programs tailored to the specific needs of government departments, private sector entities, and non-profit organizations. Our courses serve professionals involved in taxation, fiscal policy, and related fields.

We also run a robust professional development program featuring conferences, workshops, seminars, and forums. These provide spaces for open dialogue among policymakers, academics, business leaders, and government officials.

Consultancy

The Institute provides expert consultancy services on taxation law, tax policy, and fiscal policy to clients in both the public and private sectors.

Research

The Institute is dedicated to building a strong knowledge base in tax policy and law.[cite: 1] It serves as a hub for research and knowledge dissemination in taxation.

It commissions and conducts research, publishes its findings, ands host national debates, colloquiums, and seminars that bring together professionals, academics, and government officials aimed at deepening understanding of tax law and policy. Through these initiatives, it contributes to the development of a robust body of tax jurisprudence and scholarship in Kenya, while also fostering comparative insights from other jurisdictions within the East African Community and the wider global context.

Tax Policy and Law Reforms

The Institute provides actionable and practical policy advice to government and other stakeholders on current and proposed tax reforms to aid in improving policy outcomes and betterment of the tax system. The Institute plays an important role in shaping tax policy and legislative reform.

It provides a forum through which tax professionals, policymakers, and stakeholders can engage in informed dialogue on proposed tax measures, administrative practices, and broader fiscal strategies. By participating in public consultations, offering technical submissions, and conducting policy-oriented research, the Institute contributes to the development of a tax system that is equitable, efficient, and aligned with constitutional principles such as fairness, transparency, and accountability.

Public Interest Litigation

The aim of the program is to use the tax policy and law to promote social and economic justice and help disadvantaged social groups.
